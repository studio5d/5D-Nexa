Refund Policy

At 5D NEXA, we strive to provide exceptional value and satisfaction with our services. This refund policy outlines the conditions under which refunds may be granted.

Subscription Refunds

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for all new subscriptions. If you're not satisfied with our service within the first 30 days of your subscription, you can request a full refund.

Eligibility Criteria

  • The request must be made within 30 days of the initial subscription date
  • You must provide specific reasons for dissatisfaction
  • All refund requests are subject to review by our support team

One-Time Purchase Refunds

For one-time purchases such as conference tickets or premium services, refunds are available within 7 days of purchase, provided the service has not been utilized.

Non-Refundable Items

  • Services that have been fully delivered or consumed
  • Custom development or integration work
  • Third-party services or add-ons

How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please contact our support team at support@5dnexa.com with your account details and reason for the refund request. We will process eligible refunds within 5-7 business days.

Processing Time

Approved refunds will be processed to your original payment method within 5-7 business days. Please note that it may take additional time for the refund to appear in your account depending on your bank or payment provider.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this refund policy, please contact us at support@5dnexa.com or through our support page.
